The Tata Aria was built to compete with the Innova back in 2010. The Aria was a good product but Tata priced it way above the Innova back then which ultimately led to its downfall. Tata has taken the Aria apart and built an almost all-new car on the same platform with their HorizonNext design philosophy. The new Hexa is miles ahead in terms of build, quality and equipment and was first showcased to the public at Auto Expo 2016. Amongst premium people movers, the Hexa competes head to head with the Mahindra XUV500. If you do not need a seven-seater, you could also opt for a Hyundai Creta and save a bit or if you want more power, the Toyota Innova is not too far .
